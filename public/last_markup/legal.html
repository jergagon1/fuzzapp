PERSONAL SAFETY

Welcome to the FuzzFinders™ website and/or mobile application (collectively “Website”). The overwhelming majority of FuzzFinders Website users are trustworthy and well-meaning.

However, with millions of violent crimes occur in the U.S. each year, FuzzFinders urges you to take common-sense precautions when interacting with others over a lost pet.

When meeting someone for the first time, please remember to:

  • Insist on a public meeting place like a local dog park, or pet store.
  • Do not meet in a secluded place.
  • Do not invite strangers into your home.
  • Tell a friend or family member where you’re going.
  • Take your cell phone along if you have one.
  • Consider having a friend accompany you.
  • Trust your instincts.

Taking these simple precautions helps make FuzzFinders safer for everyone.

For more information about personal safety online, check out these resources:

  • www.staysafeonline.org
  • www.onguardonline.gov
  • www.getsafeonline.org
  • www.wiredsafety.org
