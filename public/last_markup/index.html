FuzzFinders is a super-intuitive app bringing people together to find lost pets.

Interactive features, collaborative mapping technology, and instant alerts. FuzzFinders is the social network empowering you to get Fuzzy back!

The FuzzFinders social network is free for everyone and allows users to:

  • Report a lost or found pet in a few simple steps.
  • Receive FuzzAlerts - Mobile alerts for missing or found pets in your area.
  • Track your lost pet's path with FuzzFinder's geotagged comments.
  • Participate in Local FuzzChat to chat with FuzzFinders in your area.
  • Earn Wags - Karma Points for helping your friends and neighbors retrieve their pets.

Lost a Pet?

It's a Pet Parent's worst nightmare! You were away for the weekend and your beloved pet escaped from the pet sitter. Getting the word out is as simple as making a post to alert your neighbors. FuzzFinders to the rescue!

Found a Pet?

You're outside and see an unfamiliar pet. It's simple. Whip out your phone and take a photo with the FuzzFinders App. Make a post to notify the owner and fuzzy gets home safe and sound. FuzzFinders to the rescue!

Earn Wags

Do you feel good when you help someone? So do we! Anytime you help a fellow FuzzFriend you earn a Wag. Wags are a currency that will change the world!
